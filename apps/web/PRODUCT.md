# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Primary: an individual managing their own money — logging transactions, checking budgets, tracking goals, and asking quick questions by voice or text. They use the app daily on a phone, sometimes on desktop, and expect it to feel as responsive as a native app.

Secondary (future): small households — couples or families — who share accounts, budgets, and goals. The data model supports this from the start, but v1 optimizes for a single user.

## Product Purpose

Prometheus is a mobile-first, multi-currency personal finance tracker and planner. It lowers the friction of daily money logging through AI-driven voice and text commands, while keeping every feature reachable through traditional forms and buttons. It is designed for self-hosting so users keep control of their financial data.

## Positioning

Voice/text-first finance that still works like a normal app.

## Brand Personality

Sharp, modern, private.

Voice and tone: direct and precise, never chatty or performative. The app should feel like a confident tool that gets out of the way — clean surfaces, fast feedback, and no unnecessary flourish. Privacy is not a feature to brag about; it is reflected in restraint, clear data boundaries, and defaults that favor user control.

## Anti-references

- A chat-only AI widget that forces conversation for every action.
- A corporate banking app: heavy, bureaucratic, overwhelming.
- A spreadsheet wrapped in a UI: dense tables, data-entry fatigue, no hierarchy.

## Design Principles

1. **Voice and text are shortcuts, not the interface.** Every AI-driven action must also be doable through forms, buttons, and familiar controls.
2. **Show the money clearly.** Multi-currency balances, budgets, and goals are presented without ambiguity — the user always knows which currency they are looking at.
3. **Mobile-first, not mobile-only.** Touch targets, bottom-sheet patterns, and voice entry are optimized for the phone; the same flows remain comfortable on desktop.
4. **Privacy through control.** Prefer local defaults, transparent AI data flow, and settings that let the user decide what leaves their server.
5. **Calm precision.** Use whitespace, type hierarchy, and intentional color to reduce anxiety around money, not to dramatize it.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. Support screen readers and keyboard navigation. Do not rely on color alone for status or actionability.
